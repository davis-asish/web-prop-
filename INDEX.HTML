My Proposal Letter

A Reassuring Promise

Sreshtha, Before I ask for forever, I want your heart to feel safe with me. I will never rush your feelings. I will never make your tears feel small. I will never let your fears face life alone. If you are tired, I will be patient. If you are worried, I will be steady. If life gets loud, I will protect your peace. You never have to be perfect with me. You only have to be yourself, and that is already enough. My love is not pressure. My love is a promise: to listen, to understand, and to stay. If you choose me, I will choose you every single day.

For You

Sreshtha, tum sirf sundar nahi ho tum woh khayal ho jo bina pooche roz mere dil mein aa jaata hai. Tumhe dekhta hoon toh duniya thodi slow ho jaati hai, jaise waqt bhi keh raha ho "ruk ja, yeh pal zaroori hai." Tumhari aankhon mein mujhe sirf khoobsurti nahi dikhti, mujhe ek ghar dikhta hai, jahan main thak ke aa sakta hoon aur bina kuch bole samjha ja sakta hoon. I don't want to love you halfway, main woh pyaar banna chahta hoon jo tumhare har darr ke aage chupchaap khada rahe. Jo tumhari khushi mein shor na kare, aur tumhare aansuon mein sabse pehle aaye. Tum meri choice nahi ho, Sreshta, tum meri dua ho. Woh dua jo maangi nahi jaati, bas mehsoos hoti hai. Agar kabhi tum thak jao, toh mera kandha tumhara rest ho. Agar duniya tumse zyada maang le, toh main tumhe yaad dilaoon ki tum kaafi ho... hamesha se. Mujhe perfect love nahi chahiye, mujhe tum chahiye. Tumhari hasi, tumhari khamoshi, tumhari har imperfect si perfect baat. Aur haan ek din, jab log poochhein "yeh kaun hai?" toh main sirf itna kahoon: "Yeh Sreshta hai. Meri mohabbat. Meri zindagi. Aur meri biwi, agar kismat ne saath diya." YOURS LOVELY DAVIS ASISH
